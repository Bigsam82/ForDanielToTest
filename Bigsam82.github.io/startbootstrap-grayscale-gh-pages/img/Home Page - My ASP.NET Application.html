At "My Closet" this app allows you to use existing clothes within the closet to make outfits or go online and pull images to create outfits. This app prevents the dreaded sitiuation of buying several articles of clothing that don't match or that don't go together well.Here at "My Closet" know how hard it is to make a great outfit work unless your Sam Saunders, but we can help you get pretty darn close!
